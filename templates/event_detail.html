The alumni talk is arranged as a platform of interaction between the alumni working in industry and current enrolled students of nutrition and dietetics. The platform provides an opportunity for the students to clear their doubts regarding the selection process and career planning.

Alumni Talk: "Wings of Dietitian" by Anjali Sindhu

The Department of Nutrition and Dietetics, UIAHS, Chandigarh University, organized an insightful alumnus talk titled "Wings of Dietitian" on 4.02.2025 at Block D7 Auditorium. Ms. Anjali Sindhu, an esteemed alumna currently working at Fitelo Pvt. Ltd.

The session aimed to provide students with valuable insights into the career prospects in nutrition and dietetics, industry expectations, and strategies to secure opportunities in leading organizations.
