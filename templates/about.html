About DBATU

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU) is a premier technological university located in Lonere, Maharashtra. Established in 1989, it offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology.

The university is dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering research and innovation. With a sprawling campus and state-of-the-art facilities, DBATU is committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers and technologists.

Lonere, situated in the picturesque Konkan region, provides a serene and conducive environment for academic pursuits. The university is well-connected by road and rail, making it accessible to students from across the country.
